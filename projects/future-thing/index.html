What is this tool?

The Thing From The Future was created by Stuart Candy and Jeff Watson of the Situation Lab at OCAD University, Toronto, Canada. There are various iterations of this tool across the Internet and this is just one that couples it with AI. The card deck is a speculative scenario generation tool for designers as a means to provide ideation and provocation to their design process. This version of the deck includes additional prompts to the original.

How to use this tool?

  • The SHUFFLE ALL button can be used to shuffle all the cards together
  • The A, T, O, or M buttons can shuffle individual Arc, Terrain, Object, and Mood cards respecitvely
  • The generation link in the Arc card can shuffle between different set generations
